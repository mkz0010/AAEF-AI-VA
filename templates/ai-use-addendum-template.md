# AI Use Addendum Template

## Purpose

This addendum describes how AI systems may be used to support vulnerability assessment activities.

## AI Usage

AI may be used for assessment planning, scope extraction, scan result organization, finding classification, report drafting, and similar support activities.

## Sensitive Data Handling

Raw credentials, API keys, secret keys, session cookies, and other high-sensitivity secrets must not be directly submitted to external AI services.

## Human Review

AI-generated outputs are not final assessment results. Findings and reports require human review before delivery.

## Customer Preference

Customer may request restrictions on external AI usage where applicable.
