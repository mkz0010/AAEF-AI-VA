Status: accepted

## Context

## Decision

~~~

## Boundaries

Model output is not authority. AI rationale is not authorization. A gate decision is not AI self-approval. Evidence supports reconstruction; it does not prove legal truth. Validator success is structural only.

Runtime demo remains necessary but deferred. Publication remains deferred. Symbol-level tracing planning is not symbol-level tracing. Keyword-level indicators are not symbol-level proof. Gap candidates are not accepted defects. No private generated outputs are moved public in v0.6.257.
