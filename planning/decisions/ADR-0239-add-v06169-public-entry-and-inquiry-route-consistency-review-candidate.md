# ADR-0239: Add v0.6.169 Public Entry and Inquiry Route Consistency Review candidate

Status: Accepted
Date: 2026-05-12

## Context

v0.6.168 selected Public Entry and Inquiry Route Consistency Review as a Medium-risk work item and assigned two checkpoints.

## Decision

Add a documentation-only Public Entry and Inquiry Route Consistency Review candidate.

The candidate reviews whether public review, commercial inquiry, and maintainer inquiry address publication routes are internally consistent and remain non-authorizing.

## Consequences

v0.6.170 should review the consistency review candidate and decide whether to close the work item.

This candidate does not create customer PoC approval, commercial contract creation, commercial license terms publication, paid engagement approval, runtime execution approval, scanner execution approval, Docker execution approval, credential use approval, customer target approval, delivery approval, AAEF main issue, or AAEF main PR.
