# Legal and NDA Considerations Draft

## Key Issues

- NDA and confidentiality obligations
- External AI service use
- Subprocessor / subcontractor treatment
- Personal data handling
- Data retention
- Data deletion
- Customer approval for AI usage
- Human review before final reporting

## Draft Position

External AI may be used only for approved purposes, with minimum necessary data, and without direct disclosure of raw credentials or high-sensitivity secrets.
