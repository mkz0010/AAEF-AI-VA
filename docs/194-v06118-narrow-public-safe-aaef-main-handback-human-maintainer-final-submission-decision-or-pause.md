## Why pause now

Pause is selected because the close-ready checklist and close-ready exact issue text candidate are sufficient internal reviewer aids.

A further move toward issue opening would require a separate human decision outside this checkpoint and should not be generated as command-like or execution-like material.

## Why keep internal now

Keep-internal is selected because the handback material remains useful for future human review while preserving:

* no direct AAEF main submission,
* no issue creation command,
* no issue URL,
* no PR path,
* no release note or document change,
* no handback package,
* no implementation detail leakage,
* no patent-sensitive detail leakage,
* no commercial or paid/NDA package leakage.

## Why not final submission now

Final submission is not selected because AI output is not authority.

The checklist being close-ready does not authorize posting.

The exact issue text candidate being close-ready does not authorize posting.

Validator output is not authority.

Only the human maintainer may independently decide to open an AAEF main issue.
